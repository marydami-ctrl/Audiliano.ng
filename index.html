About Us

AUDILIANO PROPERTIES (RC: 7312162) is a full-service real estate company providing comprehensive solutions in buying and selling properties and land, valuation, rental property supervision and management, real estate consultancy, facility management, and agency services. With over 5 years of experience, we are committed to delivering excellence and professionalism to meet the evolving needs of the real estate market.

Our Vision

To be a leading real estate company known for exceptional customer service, transparency, and innovation, offering sustainable property solutions that enhance communities and change lives.

Our Services

  • Property Sales and Acquisition: Connecting clients with prime properties and investment opportunities.
  • Property Valuation: Accurate market value assessments for properties and land.
  • Rental Property Management: Professional supervision and upkeep of rental assets.
  • Real Estate Consultancy: Expert advice tailored to individual or business property needs.
  • Facility Management: Maintenance and management of facilities for optimal functionality.
  • Agency Services: Experienced agents dedicated to fulfilling your buying, selling, and rental goals.

Our Commitment

At AUDILIANO PROPERTIES, we place a high priority on integrity, customer satisfaction, and a commitment to excellence. We understand the significance of real estate investments and strive to make the process seamless and rewarding for every client.
